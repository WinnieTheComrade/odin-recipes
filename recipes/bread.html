How To Make Great Sourdough Bread.

Ingredients

  • 1kg bread flour
  • 200gr levain
  • 650ml water
  • 20gr salt

Directions

  1. Add the levain to the water and mix it together until the levain dissolves and then add the flour and salt and give it a good mix until the dough becomes shaggy.
  2. Cover the dough and let it rest until it doubles in size and then give it a good fold for a few minutes.
  3. Let the dough rest and bulk again for 3 to 4 hours while giving it a stretch every 30 minutes.
  4. Divide the dough into two halves and gently preshape each dough into balls and let them rest uncovered for 30 minutes.
  5. Flour your work surface and dough and shape it into a batard or boule, whatever you prefer and let it rest for a few minutes.
  6. Now comes the time to put it into a lighly dusted banneton,cover it and straight overnight into the fridge.
  7. Preheat the oven at 450°F (230°C) for an hour and put the banneton into the oven for 20 minutes with steam. Remove the steaming pan and bake for another 30 minutes.
  8. Take the bread out of the oven and let it cool on a wire rack for 1 to 2 hours before cutting the bread.
