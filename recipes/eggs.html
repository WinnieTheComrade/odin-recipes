How To Make Great Scrambled Eggs.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • A pinch of salt
  • 20gr butter

Directions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk it together with the salt
  2. Heat the pan on low and add the butter
  3. Add your eggs into the pan from the bowl and swirl in circles until the eggs slighly thickens and then sweep across the pan for nice, big curds of soft and creamy eggs.
  4. Take the pan off the heat as soon as the eggs look nearly ready and let the carryover heat finish the cooking.
  5. Serve it with a dash of salt and pepper on top or add chopped shives if you feel fancy.
